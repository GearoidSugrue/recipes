Ingredients:
  - Brown pasta
  - Can of tomato soup
  - Sachet of tuna naplitina (powder used to make sauce)
  - Milk
  - Tuna (small can of shredded/chuncky tuna in brine)
  - Grated cheese

Instructions:
  1. Boil pasta in water.
  2. As pasta is boling, pour sachet into saucepan and milk and simmer.
  3. Pour tomato soup and shredded tuna chunks into the sauce.
  4. When pasta is done, drain water and add sauce into the pasta.
  5. Now put the pasta into a dish and put grated cheese on top.
  6. Put the dish in the pre-heated oven until the cheese on top has melted.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
